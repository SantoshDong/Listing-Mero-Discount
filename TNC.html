Terms and Condition

Mero Discount Card was founded in 2019 by Chakra Khadka. Chakra always to do something different and always touch millions of people and start a local search engine in Kathmandu region and thought he could do better. About 1 years later, Mero Discount Card is a way to make people life better – even if it was as simple as saving a few bucks. Thus, Mero Discount was developed to fulfill that "need". Mero Discount Card began life as Mero Discount in 2019 with 2 main aims; to help our retailers or merchandisers by encouraging people to purchase out more often, and to help our members by making enjoying out more affordable. Starting with just 50 restaurants, 25 cosmetics, 56 fashion store in Kathmandu we quickly grew and re-branded to Mero Discount Card in early 2020 as we reached 100+ nationwide restaurants, cosmetics, fashion store. As the recession bit, we gave our members the opportunity to save money without compromising on their lifestyles and helped merchandisers to continue trading in the toughest times seen for a generation. Today, with over thousands of members and thousands of merchandisers, we are proud to say that our members currently save money in millions annually in our restaurants; ensuring that our dining co-operative proposition remains strong. Mero Discount has grown hugely since our inception and is now the leading service provider in the Nepal. We remain true to our roots however, offering our members fabulous value and choice, and our merchandisers is also grow with us.
